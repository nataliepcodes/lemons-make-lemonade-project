Refreshing Lemonade Recipe

✬ Ingredients: lemons, sweetener of choice, water ✬

  • Step 1: Juice the Lemons

    Lemon is an important part of the lemonade. When life gives you lemons, welcome them!
    With acceptance. Courage. Curiosity. Grit.
    Pour your lemon juice into a serving pitcher. Serving yourself. Others.


  • Step 2: Add some Sweetness to the Lemon Juice

    Lemon is a fruit of an evergreen tree. It is sour.
    Sweeten your lemon juice with gratitude for positive things in your life. Divert negativity. Smile!😀


  • Step 3: Add some Water

    Water is vital for all forms of life. It is necessary to stay hydrated.
    Add some water into your lemon juice. Rehydrate your dreams. Find your Purpose. Discover what makes you happy.


  • Step 4: Mix your Lemonade

    Mixing is a process of combining one or more ingredients together. Until they become one unit.
    Mix your lemonade well! Explore your options. Define your goals. Try things out. Explore. Embrace mistakes. Learn.


  • Step 5: Chill your Lemonade

    Lemonade tastes better when it has been chilled.
    Refresh your mindfulness. Breathe. Focus on what is in your control. Be patient.


  • Step 6: Enjoy the Lemonade

    Remember: Lemons are important parts of the lemonade. They are not a whole lemonade.
    Enjoy. Every Moment.
